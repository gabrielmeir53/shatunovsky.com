Gabriel M. Shatunovsky

J.D. Candidate, Penn State Dickinson Law · May 2027

I am a second-year law student at Penn State Dickinson Law with interests in litigation, sports law, and administrative law. Before law school, I graduated cum laude from Florida State University with a degree in Management, where I served as Treasurer of Phi Kappa Psi, President of Students Supporting Israel, and Partnership Chair for Dance Marathon.

During the summer of 2025, I worked as a Summer Associate at Carman, Callahan & Ingham, LLP, a litigation firm on Long Island, where I drafted motions for summary judgment, consolidation, and to compel, along with legal memoranda and mediation submissions used in active cases. I also shadowed attorneys across multiple New York state courts in the Bronx, Suffolk County, Nassau County, and Manhattan. That experience solidified my interest in litigation and gave me a practical understanding of how legal strategy translates from the page to the courtroom.

At Dickinson Law, I serve as the 2L Class Representative for the Student Bar Association and as President of the Jewish Law Student Association. My academic work has focused on the intersection of law with the structures that govern professional sports and federal regulation. I am bilingual in English and Russian.
